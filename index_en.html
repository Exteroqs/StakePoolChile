Supporting network decentralization

I have my server in Chile, because a more decentralized network is more resistant. In addition, the servers have low energy consumption thanks to their 16-core ARM processor and together with their SSD disks in raid0, it gives them greater writing and reading speeds. Support me with your delegation, it will allow me to keep this Stake Pool running :)

Low commissions by the Pool

The pool only obtains 1.8% of the profits, the rest is distributed to its delegates. The fixed fee charged will be to cover maintenance costs and improve the equipment's hardware, these changes will be notified in the pool activity or on twitter.

Who I am

I am a veteran operator, I started participating in the test network (ITN) in December 2019 and I was one of the first pools in South America to participate. I take my job as an operator seriously and I am constantly informing myself of new changes in the network as well as applying the corresponding updates to my nodes in a timely manner. I can say that I am a person who likes transparency, so in the face of any problem you will be notified by twitter, which is my communication tool at this time.
