The name Tricahue

The tricahue, named locally in the Mapuche language, is a subspecies Cyanoliseus and is an endemic bird of Chile, this little friend can be found from Atacama to Valdivia. They are very sociable and affectionate animals among them, but misleading with strangers. In the 90s, this bird was on the verge of extinction, thanks to the effort to have conservation areas for these birds after 11 years, its population has increased again concentrating mainly in the central area of ​​Chile.
